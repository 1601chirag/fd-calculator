FD Calculator

This FD calculator helps you calculate the maturity amount of a fixed deposit easily. It is useful for understanding how your savings grow when invested in a fixed deposit.

Fixed Deposit (FD) is a type of investment where you deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed period at a fixed rate of interest.

How FD Maturity Amount is Calculated

The maturity amount of a fixed deposit is calculated using the compound interest formula:

Maturity Amount = Principal × (1 + Rate / 100)Time

This calculator uses the same formula to give accurate FD maturity results instantly.

Example of Fixed Deposit Calculation

If you invest ₹50,000 in a fixed deposit at an interest rate of 6% per year for 5 years:

Maturity Amount = 50,000 × (1 + 6 / 100)5 = ₹66,911 (approx)
